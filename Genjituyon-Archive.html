Universe Gazing

Gazing out into the vast and open snowy hills, flickering light from the bonfires of Point Cosmodent in the distance, Lilac Mausu and Salix Coyote, a kosmitsune using fire magic to keep himself warm in the sub zero climate, were sitting up ontop of a hill looking up at the universe filled sky."I've never seen the night sky filled with so many stars before, all because Trillium is pretty strict on scheduling." he said, readjusting to a more comfortable sitting position. Lilac looked over to Salix, head shaking with a sigh that then tranfered into a quaint little chuckle, "each of those stars is an individual universe, and they're all unique." She then pointed to several different points in the sky, "Those universes are the ones we're currently gathering the most data about."Salix looked at Lilac with confusion,"data such as what?" He wanted to know some of the data they've accumulated from these worlds, but Lilac just gave a smug look and replied, "Unfortunately, it's all classified until enough data is gathered to publically release it."
